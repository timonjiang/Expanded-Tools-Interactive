3 Favorite Websites or Personal Project and Why

Timon (gerund+adjective+noun)

http://njla.us/

The design of the web page represents the creativity and entertaining spirit of the studio as a design consultancy. Viewers navigate the web page by the special design of the mouse and feel like in the interstellar game. The design also plays with the pixel graphics element to enhance the entertaining feeling. I can find surprise details and ideas when interacting with the website. The website itself is a design project, and it feels fun to explore the work by the studio in this way.
